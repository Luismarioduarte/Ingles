Global Environmental Degradation

Deforestation is a major sub-category of environmental degradation, threatening global climate stability.

• Anthropogenic Climate Change
• Ecosystem Services Depletion
• Global Biodiversity Loss

Key Characteristics

Defined by permanent removal of forest cover, resulting in long-term structural landscape shifts.

• Permanent Canopy Loss
• Fragmented Habitats
• Disrupted Nutrient Cycling
Concept Forestry Loss

Exclusions

Activities that do not imply permanent destruction of the forest ecosystem.

• Sustainable Silviculture
• Natural Regeneration
• Reforestation Programs

Local Manifestations

Specific illegal and economic activities targeting strategic Colombian ecosystems.

• Unregulated Cattle Ranching
• Illegal Timber Extraction
• Agricultural Frontier Expansion
